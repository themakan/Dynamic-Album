Hawaii 2015

Spring break 2015, the Dullabh and Makan families head to Kona, Hawaii at a resort called Kukio where we are renting a home together. Divesh leaves on Thursday evening with the girls - the first trip he is taking alone with them. I join the next morning. After rushing to pick them up from school, quick snack and ready for the car to the airport, the house is quiet. An eery quiet. I get ready for my FEWD class and head out. The quiet is almost noisly disturbing.
